Understanding 3D Laser Scanning:

Before delving into Iscano's contributions, it's essential to grasp the fundamentals of 3D laser scanning. Unlike traditional surveying methods, which rely on manual measurements and are time-consuming, 3D laser scanning employs laser beams to capture millions of data points in seconds. These data points create a detailed, three-dimensional representation of the scanned object or environment, offering unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.
